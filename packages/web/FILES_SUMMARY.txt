API ENDPOINTS USED
================================================================================

GET /api/businesses
  - Params: search?, category?, city?, page?, limit?
  - Returns: ApiResponse<Business[]>

GET /api/businesses/{id}
  - Returns: Business

GET /api/jobs
  - Params: search?, type?, city?, h1b_sponsor?, opt_friendly?, page?, limit?
  - Returns: ApiResponse<Job[]>

GET /api/jobs/{id}
  - Returns: Job

GET /api/news
  - Params: search?, category?, city?, page?, limit?
  - Returns: ApiResponse<NewsArticle[]>

GET /api/news/{id}
  - Returns: NewsArticle

GET /api/events
  - Params: category?, city?, page?, limit?
  - Returns: ApiResponse<DesiEvent[]>

GET /api/deals
  - Params: city?, page?, limit?
  - Returns: ApiResponse<Deal[]>

GET /api/consultancies
  - Params: specialization?, city?, page?, limit?
  - Returns: ApiResponse<Consultancy[]>
